Platform Intelligence FAQ

Everything you need to know about the CuratorAI ecosystem.

How does the AI synthesis engine work? expand_more
Our engine uses multi-agent neural architectures to cross-reference your data points against 40+ strategic frameworks. It doesn't just summarize; it identifies structural gaps and emergent opportunities in your research.
Can I export my curated intelligence? expand_more
Absolutely. We support high-fidelity exports to PDF, Markdown, JSON, and direct integrations with Notion, Slack, and professional CMS platforms. Your wisdom remains portable and sovereign.
How secure is my proprietary data? expand_more
We employ zero-knowledge architecture. All synthesis happens in isolated environments with end-to-end encryption. CuratorAI is SOC2 Type II compliant and never trains base models on user data.

The Core and Zenith plans include advanced multiplayer functionality, allowing teams to build shared intelligence graphs, leave contextual feedback, and co-curate high-impact strategic reports in real-time.
